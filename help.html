Hello! This website is created to help you convert your text files (epub, pdf, word) into mp3 files! By default this website is presented in English, if you prefer French not to worry, there is also a French option available by clicking on "Français" accessible on all the pages.

You can convert your files by uploading them under the "Upload Your File" tab. The file can either be dropped within the parameter of the drop dox or by clicking the "Choose a File" button to upload your text files.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding the website please feel free to contact us by clicking the "Contact Us" tab.
